CONVOCATORIA 

 


The Casa Tres Patios is collaborating with Cittadellarte-Fondazione Pistoletto for the pre-selection of candidates from Latin America for the UNIDEE in Residence programme that will take place from 15th June to15th October 2011 in Biella, Italy. The candidates need to send the applications via email to bothCasa Tres Patios and UNIDEE before midnight January 15th 2011. Applications received after this date will not be considered.

casatrespatios1@gmail.com
unidee@cittadellarte.it 

The candidates have the opportunity to apply to different grants, the Fondo Edo Tempia Grant (partially funded) and Fondazione Zegna Grant (fully funded). Please refer to the link for full information

http://www.cittadellarte.it/progetti.php?prog=28 
 
All questions from Latin American artists should be directed to the Casa Tres Patios at the above email address.
