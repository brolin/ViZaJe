camikant (sos.cantor@gmail.com) would like to invite you to
try Unlibrary.com - a free system for organizing and sharing your books - and
join their community "camikant's friends".

With Unlibrary, you can quickly catalog and organize your books -
simply enter a title or ISBN, and the rest is found for you.
But the real power comes with the ability to create communities of
people and share your collections. Unlibrary is being used all over the
world in schools, workplaces, churches, and groups of friends, to pool
resources and share a love of reading.

To join or find out more, follow this link -
http://www.unlibrary.com

Once you sign up, you'll see an invitation on your home page to join camikant's community.

Look forward to having you on board!
