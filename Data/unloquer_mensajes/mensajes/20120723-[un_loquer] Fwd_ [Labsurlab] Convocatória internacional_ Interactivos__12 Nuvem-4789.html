Interactivos?'12 Nuvem
Autonomics: rural science
http://nuvem.tk/?autonomias/

NUVEM
rural art
technology center

In partnership with Medialab-Prado, Nuvem will select 5 proposals for a 
residency at our rural hacklab in Visconde de Mauá (Rio de Janeiro, Brazil) 
between November 1st and November 17th, 2012. Before the 18th of August, 
researchers, professionals and amateurs are invited to send projects dealing 
with communications, agriculture, food, drinking water, energy, transportation, 
bio-construction, from a rural perspective. Selected projects will be awarded 
transportation, housing, limited financial and technical support for the development 
of the proposals.

Open call till august 18th, 2012
